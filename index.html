The Real Problem

The India PM prep market has a structural failure — not a content gap.

T1

Preparation Fragmentation

The average Indian PM candidate uses 4–7 disconnected platforms. No single source provides what they need. The cost is misdirected effort — not lack of motivation.

T2

Roadmap Void

"Jobs before skills" — candidates apply before they know what to learn. No platform maps background → specific PM role type → personalised preparation path.

T3

Feedback Void

After rejection, candidates receive nothing. After practice, they receive nothing. After months of effort, they have no signal of progress. Repetition without feedback is not preparation.

Core Features

Built differently — on purpose.

🔍

State Detection Engine

A 10-minute adaptive diagnostic that detects where you actually are in your journey — not where Day 0 was. Outputs a personalised Starting Point Card. Mid-journey candidates are never shown content they've already covered.

🧭

Role Discovery Path

Four India PM archetypes — Technical PM, Growth PM, Consumer PM, AI PM — mapped to your background, instincts, and target company type. Comes with India salary ranges and target employers.

📋

Weekly Mission

Your default home screen is never a curriculum. It's one case (15 min) + one concept (10 min) + one action (5 min). Completable in a Tuesday evening. No streak pressure.

⚡

Simulation Engine — No AI Zone

India-contextualised case simulations (Swiggy, CRED, Razorpay, Zepto) in conditions that mirror real GCC and mid-size PM interviews. No AI assistance available during sessions — because interviews don't allow it.

💬

Directional Feedback

Every simulation ends with 4 specific statements: what you did well, where your thinking broke down, why it matters for your specific role type, and exactly what to do in the next session.

🎯

Readiness Signal — 3 States Only

Performance-based, not completion-based. Building → Getting Close → Ready. When you're Ready, you get a time-bounded instruction: "Apply to 3–5 roles this week." No percentages. No perfectionism loops.

What PMReady Is NOT

Some design decisions are constraints, not features.

🚫

No content feeds or course modules. You have 73 bookmarks and 4 half-finished courses. More content is not what you need. A directed action is.

🚫

No streaks, no badges, no leaderboards. You're a working professional with irregular hours. Missed Tuesday ≠ failure. Streak mechanics punish the most dedicated users.

🚫

No percentage readiness scores. "78% ready" creates a permanent focus on the 18%. Three states only — Building, Getting Close, Ready — so perfectionism has nowhere to hide.

🚫

No paywall before first simulation. You've already paid for 3 platforms you didn't finish. This product earns your trust before asking for payment.
